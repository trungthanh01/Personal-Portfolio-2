About Me

Hi, I'm Marcus, a passionate frontend developer with a love for creating beautiful and functional web applications. Beside that, I have a keen interest in cryptocurrency, AI and reading book. I enjoy exploring new technologies and continuously improving my skills. Some of my favorite topics: Frontend, Build In Public, Indie Hacker, Blockchain, Chainlink, Worldcoin, History.
Well I started In Hospitality and have been working as a Guest Experience Expert, where I focused on enhancing customer satisfaction and ensuring a seamless user experience. But It was not my passion, I chose Hospitality because I had no idea what I should learn. I didn't know my hobby and I got stucked in someone's decision. So I decided to split some hours/day change my path and become a Web Designer. I have set my roadmap to become an Indie Hacker that I can create my own products and services. I know that a long-term goal requires short-term goals, so I am currently focusing on learning the basics of web development and building my portfolio by HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
I am excited to share my journey with you and hope to inspire others to pursue their passions and dreams. And yessss, It is fucking hard. The question is "Do you actually living your life or just existing?" I have been asking myself that question for a long time. But I know one thing for sure: I want to live my life to the fullest. Just keep going bro!!
